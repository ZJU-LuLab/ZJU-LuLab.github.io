Dr. Lu Lu is currently an assistant professor in the Department of Engineering Mechanics at Zhejiang University. Our research combines theoretical modeling, numerical simulation, and experiments to understand the nonlinear deformation, instability, and shape transformation in slender structures, soft materials, and reconfigurable mechanical systems. 

## Research Interests

- Mechanical instabilities, including buckling, snap-through, bifurcation, and post-buckling behavior
- Soft active materials, including liquid crystal elastomers, magnetic soft materials, and stimuli-responsive polymers
- Elastic rods, rings, plates, and shells
- Deployable, reconfigurable, and morphing structures
- Mechanics-guided design for soft robotics and functional materials
